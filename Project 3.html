The begginign

Going to the gym at the very beginning of your fitness journey can be a very hard step
you can easily start training at home
Here is a short and easy beginner daily routine that you can follow
Bodyweight Squats - 3 sets of 10-15 reps
Push-Ups (knee or standard) - 3 sets of 8-12 reps
Plank - 3 sets of 20-30 seconds
On top of this you can also add some cardio if you have time for it
